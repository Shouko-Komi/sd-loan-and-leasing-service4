Cooling-off Period for Loans & Leasing: What You Need to Know

Did you know that when you sign a loan or leasing agreement, you may have a **cooling-off period**? This allows you to **reconsider the financial commitment** before fully committing. At SD Loans and Leasing, we ensure that clients understand their financial rights before signing any agreements.

How the Cooling-off Period Works for Loans

Depending on the **type of loan**, different cooling-off periods apply:

  • Personal Loans: Usually a **14-day period** applies.
  • Car Loans: Some states offer **a 1- to 2-day window**.
  • Home Loans: Generally, a **3- to 5-day period** applies.
